開發者資訊 Developer Profile

作者：鄭郁翰 (Yu-Han Cheng) Created by Yu-Han Cheng

身為受偏頭痛所苦的開發者，我深刻理解每一次發作帶來的痛苦與不便。因此，我以「最少點擊、最大價值」為核心打造了這款「完全本機儲存、零外部追蹤」的健康管理工具。希望能為您提供最純粹、最安全的偏頭痛紀錄體驗，並在您就診諮詢時提供真正有價值的診斷參考。 As a developer who personally battles chronic migraines, I understand the pain and frustration each attack brings. This motivated me to build this utility under the philosophy of "minimal clicks, maximum value, and absolute privacy" with zero tracking. I hope it helps you manage your symptoms effortlessly and provide meaningful references for your next doctor's visit.
